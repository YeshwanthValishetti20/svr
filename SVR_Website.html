Our journey

From farm fields
to Film Nagar's main road.

We didn't start with capital. We started with two hands, a share auto, and rice from the farms we grew up near.

2003
From village fields to Hyderabad's roads
Farming background, Nalgonda district. When the village could no longer hold us, we came to the city. Drove borewell vehicles, road rollers, lorries. Joined Jubilee Public School as a driver at ₹3,000/month. Later moved to Oakridge International School.
Night
Midnight auto rides & a wife who never stopped
Started driving share auto evenings for second income — till midnight every night. My wife did daily labour and domestic work alongside. Together, penny by penny, we turned a small hut into our home.
Idea
The rice from Marrigudem
My wife's family grows rice near Miryalaguda, Nalgonda — fed by Nagarjuna Sagar water. Pure Sona Masoori. Every neighbour who tasted it asked: "Where do you get this?" That question was the seed of everything.
5 yrs
Every Sunday. Share auto. My son beside me.
Connected with my wife's brother-in-law near Nakrekal — he had rice mill connections. Every Sunday morning: load 5 quintals, drive back to Hyderabad, sell from one room in the house. Did this for 5 years. Volume grew to 40 quintals. The mill started delivering to our door.
2022
One phone call. One shutter. One name.
My brother called: "The shutter beside my shop is available." We met the owner. We agreed. Sri Venkateshwara Rice Traders — main road, opposite Current Office, Film Nagar — opened its doors.
Today
500+ families. 19 brands. 3 years.
From Sona Masoori only, to 19 premium brands — each hand-selected. From 5 quintals in a share auto to supplying hotels, caterers and retail shops across Hyderabad. Same values. Same promise. Made for Quality.
"I came a long way to reach here. Every step — the lorry, the auto, the Sunday mornings with my son — was worth it. Because every customer who trusts us trusts our journey too."
